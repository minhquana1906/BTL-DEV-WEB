Reservations for Gordon Ramsay HELL’S KITCHEN are now available for booking online. HELL’S KITCHEN will open to the public on Saturday, July 15 at 4:30pm.

Foxwoods has officially announced the opening of Gordon Ramsay HELL’S KITCHEN at the property this summer, inspired by the hit television series.

The restaurant will be located in the Grand Pequot Tower and will bring the celebrated chef’s signature dishes and dining experience to Foxwoods’ vast selection of global culinary options for guests. The eatery will seat nearly 260 guests, featuring a chef’s counter and 40-seat private dining room.

Featuring design elements that draw from the hit television show, the new restaurant will offer guests an immersive dining experience that combines the energy and excitement of a busy kitchen with upscale comfort and sophistication. The menu will feature signature classics from the show, including Beef Wellington and Sticky Toffee Pudding, as well as an extensive wine list, craft cocktails, and a carefully curated selection of beers and spirits, all designed to complement the bold and flavorful dishes on the menu.

For groups larger than seven guests, please reach out to our team at (860) 312-6455 or email us at Bpuma@foxwoods.com
